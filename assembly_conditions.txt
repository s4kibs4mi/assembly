ASSEMBLY LANGUAGE CODITIONS

SIGNED

JG/JNLE : GREATER THAN
JGE/JNL : GREATER EQUAL
JL/JNGE : LESS THAN
JLE/JNG : LESS THAN EQUAL


UNSIGNED

JA/JNBE : ABOVE
JAE/JNB : ABOVE OR EQUAL
JB/JNAE : BELOW
JBE/JNA : EQUAL

SINGLE-FLAG JUMPS

JE/JZ   : EQUAL
JNE/JNZ : NOT EQUAL
JC      : CARRY
JNC     : NOT CARRY
JO      : OVERFLOW
JNO     : NO OVERFLOW
JS      : SIGN NEGATIVE
JNS     : NON-NEGATIVE SIGN
JP/JPE  : PARITY EVEN
JNP/JPO : PARITY ODD
